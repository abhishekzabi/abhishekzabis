About Me

My name is Abhishek KP, and I am a web designer, UX designer, and illustrator based in Kannur, Kerala. I have always been passionate about design and technology, and I love creating beautiful and functional digital experiences that delight users.

In my work, I use a variety of tools and techniques to bring my ideas to life, including Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Procreate. I am constantly learning and experimenting with new design trends and technologies, and I enjoy collaborating with other designers and developers to create innovative and impactful solutions.

If you are looking for a talented and dedicated designer who can help you bring your vision to life, please feel free to get in touch with me. I would love to hear from you and discuss your design needs and goals. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to working with you!
